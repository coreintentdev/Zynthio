## Playlist Submission Strategy

### Spotify Editorial (Highest Priority)
- **Submit via:** Spotify for Artists dashboard → "Pitch a Song"
- **Lead time required:** Minimum 7 days before release date (14+ recommended)
- **What to include:**
  - Genre and mood tags (be specific — e.g. "late night electronic" not just "electronic")
  - Instruments used
  - Storyline / concept behind the track
  - Target audience description
- **Tip:** Submit on a Monday or Tuesday — editorial teams review mid-week

### Independent Playlist Curators
Submit to curators via these platforms (no cost):
- **SubmitHub** — pay-per-submission model, high-quality gatekeeping
- **Groover** — EU-focused but global reach
- **Musosoup** (UK-based, NZ artists accepted)
- **Playlist Push** — algorithmic + human curators

**Target playlist genres for DJ Zynrose:**
- Electronic / EDM
- Chill Electronic
- Deep House / Tech House (if applicable)
- New Zealand Electronic (niche but loyal)
- Gaming / Focus / Study playlists (high stream counts, algo-friendly)

### YouTube & SoundCloud
- Upload stems/snippet as a "teaser" on YouTube Shorts (30–60 sec)
- Submit full track to SoundCloud reposts via Groover or direct curator DMs
- Tag `#NZMusic` `#DJZynrose` `#SongPal` on all posts

## Social Media Release Plan Template

Copy and customise for each release.

### T-minus 14 Days (Announce)
- **Instagram / TikTok:** Short clip of studio session or cover art reveal — 15–30 sec
- **Caption template:**
  > New track incoming. [TITLE] drops [DATE]. Pre-save link in bio. #DJZynrose #SongPal #NZMusic #Electronic
- **Action:** Enable pre-save via DistroKid HyperFollow

### T-minus 7 Days (Build)
- **Instagram Story:** Poll — "Which vibe do you prefer?" (use audio snippets)
- **TikTok:** Behind-the-scenes clip — production process, DAW screen
- **Twitter/X:** Thread about the concept or inspiration for the track

### T-minus 3 Days (Push)
- **Instagram / TikTok:** 60-second snippet with lyrics overlay (if applicable)
- **Email list / SongPal users:** Early access or exclusive preview link

### Release Day
- **Instagram / TikTok / YouTube Shorts:** Full cover art + link in bio post
- **Caption template:**
  > [TITLE] is out now. Stream on Spotify, Apple Music, and everywhere else. Link in bio. #DJZynrose #NewMusic #SongPal
- **Stories:** Add "Stream Now" sticker with link
- **Twitter/X:** Direct Spotify link post

### T-plus 7 Days (Sustain)
- **Instagram Reel / TikTok:** Fan reaction or lyric video if applicable
- **Submit to additional playlists** (SubmitHub second wave)
- **Spotify for Artists:** Check stream data — pitch again if performing well

### T-plus 30 Days (Review)
- Pull streaming analytics from DistroKid + Spotify for Artists
- Note what worked — add to this document for next release

## Platform-Specific Optimisation

### Spotify
- **Profile:** Keep bio updated; include SongPal mention
- **Canvas:** Upload a looping 3–8 second video (MP4, 720×1280) via Spotify for Artists
- **Artist's Pick:** Pin the latest single to your profile
- **Algorithmic levers:**
  - Stream saves and playlist adds drive Discovery Weekly and Radio
  - Encourage "Save" and "Add to playlist" in captions — not just "stream"
- **Release Radar:** Followers automatically get new releases here — grow followers via cross-platform

### Apple Music
- **Claim profile** via Apple Music for Artists
- **Upload press photo** and biography (keep it concise, 150–200 words)
- **Lyrics:** Upload synced lyrics to DistroKid for automatic Apple Music display
- **No Canvas equivalent** — strong cover art matters more here
- **Shazam integration:** Happens automatically; ensure metadata is correct

### YouTube Music
- **Claim via YouTube Studio** (link your YouTube channel)
- **Official Artist Channel (OAC):** Apply once you have distributed content live
- **Upload lyric video** or visualiser to YouTube itself — this feeds YouTube Music
- **Shorts strategy:** 60-second vertical cuts of the track with #Shorts tag
- **Monetisation:** Enable Content ID via DistroKid (generates revenue from covers/uses)

### TikTok / Instagram Reels
- Track is automatically added to TikTok's sound library via DistroKid
- **Encourage UGC:** Ask followers to use the sound; respond to creators who do
- **Hook:** First 3 seconds of the clip must be compelling — no slow intros
