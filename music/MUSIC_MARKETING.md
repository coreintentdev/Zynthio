## 1. Playlist Submission Strategy

### Spotify Editorial (Official Playlists)
- Submit via **Spotify for Artists** at least **7 days before release date** (earlier is better — 3–4 weeks ideal)
- Pitch only **one track per release** — choose your strongest single
- Pitch fields to complete:
  - Genre and subgenre (be specific — e.g. "Electronic / Melodic Techno")
  - Mood (e.g. energetic, dark, euphoric)
  - Instrumentation (synthesiser, drum machine, vocals, etc.)
  - Storyline / artist context (1–2 sentences in plain language)
  - City and country: **New Zealand**
  - Tour dates (if any — boosts consideration)

### Independent Playlist Curators
Submit to independent curators **4–6 weeks before release**. Target services:

| Service | Notes |
|---------|-------|
| SubmitHub | Paid credits, curator feedback, direct pitching |
| Groover | European focus, paid, high curator quality |
| Playlist Push | US-focused, higher cost, good reach |
| Daily Playlists | Free tier available |
| PlaylistSupply | NZ / AU curator network — prioritise for regional reach |

**Curator outreach email template:**

```
Subject: New Track Pitch — DJ Zynrose "[Track Name]" [Genre] — Release [Date]

Hi [Curator Name],

I'm DJ Zynrose, an electronic music producer based in New Zealand. 
I have a new track "[Track Name]" releasing on [Date] that I think 
fits your [Playlist Name] playlist.

The track is [1–2 sentence description — energy, mood, influences].

Pre-save link: [HyperFollow URL]
Streaming preview: [private SoundCloud / Dropbox link]

Thanks for your time and consideration.

Corey McIvor (DJ Zynrose)
SongPal | New Zealand
```

### Own Playlist Strategy
- Maintain 2–3 branded playlists on Spotify:
  - **"DJ Zynrose: Best Of"** — own tracks only
  - **"SongPal: [Genre] Selections"** — own tracks + curated similar artists
  - **"DJ Zynrose: In The Studio"** — unreleased snippets, demos, inspirations
- Add new releases to own playlists immediately on release day
- Collaborate on playlist swaps with NZ/AU producers of similar genre

## 3. Platform-Specific Optimisation

### Spotify
- **Profile:** Keep Spotify for Artists profile 100% complete (bio, photos, links)
- **Canvas:** Upload a looping 3–8 second video Canvas for every track (accepted formats: MP4, vertical, 9:16)
- **Artist Pick:** Pin latest release or most strategic track at top of profile
- **Playlist:** Maintain own playlists — Spotify's algorithm rewards active curators
- **Presave campaigns:** Use DistroKid HyperFollow or feature.fm
- **Spotify Wrapped:** Track major milestones (streams, listeners, saves) monthly
- **Marquee:** DistroKid or direct Spotify Marquee ad for major releases (paid, targets followers of similar artists)

### Apple Music
- **Apple Music for Artists:** Claim profile — update bio, photos, social links
- **Shazam integration:** Shazam auto-populates from Apple Music; no extra action needed but monitor Shazam charts
- **iTunes Connect:** Not required (DistroKid handles delivery)
- **MusicKit:** Consider embedding Apple Music player on SongPal website
- **Lyrics:** Upload synced lyrics via DistroKid to enable Apple Music animated lyrics
- **Apple Music Radio:** Pitch to Beats 1 / Apple Music NZ editorial for feature consideration (via email to Apple editorial team)

### YouTube Music
- **YouTube Official Artist Channel (OAC):** Claim via DistroKid or YouTube for Artists — merges uploads with auto-generated music content
- **YouTube channel content:**
  - Upload official audio/lyric video for every release (separate from auto-generated)
  - Create official music video (even lo-fi visualiser) — YouTube rewards owned uploads
  - Shorts: 15–60 sec clips around release campaigns
- **Metadata optimisation:**
  - Title format: `DJ Zynrose — [Track Name] (Official Audio)`
  - Description: full track info, lyrics snippet, buy/stream links, social links
  - Tags: artist name, genre, mood, BPM, key, similar artists
  - Custom thumbnail: consistent branding, 1280×720 minimum
- **YouTube Music pitch:** No direct editorial pitch — algorithmic. Focus on watch time, saves, and engagement.

### TikTok
- **Sound registration:** DistroKid distributes to TikTok; monitor use of your sound
- **Hook-forward tracks:** Ensure the catchiest 15 seconds work as a standalone clip
- **Creator outreach:** DM 5–10 mid-tier TikTok creators in your genre to use your sound
- **Trending sounds:** Use TikTok Creative Center to identify trending audio styles to inform production
- **DJ Zynrose TikTok:** Post 3–5× per week during release campaigns; use own sound in videos

### SoundCloud
- **For demos/previews:** Use SoundCloud for pre-release sharing with curators (private links)
- **Repost network:** Engage with NZ/AU electronic music reposter accounts
- **Free downloads:** Gate downloads with email capture (SoundCloud Pro feature)

### Bandcamp
- **For merch + direct sales:** If physical or high-res downloads are planned, Bandcamp is optimal
- **Friday releases:** Bandcamp Fridays (first Friday of month) = no platform fee; maximise revenue
- **NZ audience:** Bandcamp has strong indie/electronic community — good for regional fans
