## 1. Playlist Submission Strategy

### Timeline

| Weeks Before Release | Action |
|----------------------|--------|
| 8+ weeks | Submit to editorial playlist curators (Spotify, Apple) |
| 6 weeks | Submit to independent playlist curators (SubmitHub, Groover) |
| 4 weeks | Pitch to blog/press outlets for premiere or feature |
| 2 weeks | Submit to user-generated playlists (Spotify playlist curator communities) |
| Release week | Post on social media, engage with streams/saves |
| 2–4 weeks post | Follow up on playlist adds; submit to genre-specific curators |

---

### Spotify Editorial Playlists

- Submit via **Spotify for Artists** → Music → Upcoming → Pitch to Playlist
- Can only pitch **one unreleased track per release** at a time
- Submit minimum **7 days before release date** (earlier is better)
- Write a compelling pitch: genre, mood, instruments, story behind the track
- Target playlists relevant to DJ Zynrose's sound (electronic, dance, ambient, etc.)

**Pitch Template:**
```
[TRACK NAME] is a [genre] track built around [core instrument/element].
The mood is [descriptor(s)] — designed for [context: e.g. late-night driving,
club warm-up, focused work]. The production draws from [influences/style].
Based in New Zealand, DJ Zynrose is releasing this as part of [single/EP/album].
```

---

### Independent Playlist Curators

#### SubmitHub (submithub.com)
- Use for blog premieres AND playlist placements
- Filter by genre (electronic, dance, ambient, etc.)
- Budget: $0.50–$2 per submission credit (bulk credits available)
- Target curators with response rate > 60%

#### Groover (groover.co)
- European-heavy curator network
- Good for electronic / underground / bass music genres
- Budget: €2–€5 per curator

#### Indiemono, Awesome New Indie, Submit Hub alternatives
- Free or low-cost playlists via community submissions
- Search Reddit r/SpotifyPlaylists, r/ThisIsOurMusic

---

### Apple Music Editorial

- Submit via **Apple Music for Artists** → Submit to Apple Music Editors
- Available after release goes live (no pre-release editorial pitching tool)
- Requires apple.com/music artist page to be active

## 3. Platform-Specific Optimisation

### Spotify

#### Profile Optimisation
- [ ] Artist bio: 1–3 sentences, first-person, mentions genre and NZ origin
- [ ] Artist photo: high-res, consistent with visual brand
- [ ] Pick an "Artist's Pick" (featured track or playlist) — update monthly
- [ ] Create a public "DJ Zynrose Radio" playlist with own tracks + influences
- [ ] Enable Spotify Canvas for each track (8-second looping MP4, 720×1280px)
- [ ] Enable Spotify Clips (short artist video, up to 30 seconds)

#### Canvas Specs
- File: MP4 or JPEG sequence
- Resolution: 720 × 1280 px (portrait/vertical)
- Duration: 3–8 seconds (loops seamlessly)
- File size: < 10 MB
- No logos, handles, or text overlays (Spotify policy)

#### Pre-Save Campaign
- Use tools: **ToneDen**, **Feature.fm**, **Hypeddit**, or **Distrokid's HyperFollow**
- HyperFollow (free with DistroKid) creates a pre-save + all-platforms link automatically
- Share the HyperFollow link in all pre-release social posts

#### Algorithmic Playlists to Target
- Release Radar (auto — triggered by followers saving/streaming)
- Discover Weekly (requires listener engagement and saves)
- Daily Mix (requires repeat listening by existing fans)
- Radio stations (artist radio — improves with listener saves)

---

### Apple Music

#### Profile Optimisation
- [ ] Claim artist profile on **Apple Music for Artists**
- [ ] Upload artist photo (3000×3000px recommended, square)
- [ ] Write artist bio
- [ ] Create an Apple Music playlist of own tracks

#### Metadata for Apple Music
- Apple is strict on metadata — double-check all fields at submission
- Apple requires proper composer / publisher info for sync opportunities
- Apple Music Spatial Audio: if your DAW supports Dolby Atmos mixdown, enable spatial audio delivery via DistroKid

#### Spatial Audio (Optional but High Value)
- Dolby Atmos mixdown: export as ADM BWF from compatible DAW
- Adds "Spatial Audio" badge on Apple Music — improves discoverability
- DistroKid supports Spatial Audio delivery for Apple Music

---

### YouTube Music

#### YouTube Channel Setup
- [ ] YouTube channel: name matches `DJ Zynrose`
- [ ] Channel art consistent with SongPal / DJ Zynrose branding
- [ ] Upload official audio or visualiser video for each track
- [ ] Enable YouTube Content ID via DistroKid (monetises covers of your tracks)

#### Video Content Strategy
- **Visualiser:** Static or animated artwork + waveform (easy to produce)
- **Lyric video:** For tracks with lyrics — increases engagement
- **Making-of / studio session:** Behind the scenes — strong on YouTube Shorts
- **Live set / DJ mix:** If applicable — builds audience

#### YouTube Shorts
- 15–60 second vertical clips from the track
- Best performing hook, build, or drop
- Hashtags: `#DJZynrose #Shorts #[Genre] #NewMusic`

#### YouTube SEO
- Title format: `[Track Name] — DJ Zynrose (Official Audio)`
- Description: full metadata block + social links + Spotify/Apple links
- Tags: artist name, genre, mood, BPM, key, release year
- Thumbnail: cover art at 1280×720px (YouTube crops from 3000×3000)

---

### TikTok / Instagram Reels

- Both platforms use the same vertical video format (1080×1920px)
- DistroKid can deliver tracks to TikTok's sound library (included in most plans)
- When your track is in TikTok's sound library, users can use it in their own videos → organic reach
- Monitor TikTok Creator Marketplace for potential influencer placements
