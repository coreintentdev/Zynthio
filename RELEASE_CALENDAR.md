## Release Frequency Guidelines

| Format | Recommended Cadence |
|--------|---------------------|
| Singles | Every 4–8 weeks (builds algorithmic momentum) |
| EPs | 2–3 per year |
| Albums | 1 per year maximum |
| DJ Mixes | Monthly (SoundCloud / Mixcloud — not distributed) |

**Note:** Consistent release cadence is more important than large gaps between projects. Single releases keep your profile active in algorithms.

---

## Blackout Dates (Avoid Releasing)

These periods have high competition and lower organic discovery:

| Period | Reason |
|--------|--------|
| December 20 – January 5 | Holiday noise — major label album dumps |
| New Year's Eve weekend | Listener attention is elsewhere |
| Major award show weeks | News cycle dominates |

---

## Strategic Release Timing

| Day of Week | Notes |
|-------------|-------|
| Friday | Industry standard — playlists refresh, editorial picks go live |
| Wednesday | Mid-week drop — can stand out with less competition |
| Avoid Monday–Tuesday | Lowest new music discovery activity |

**New Zealand Release Note:** NZ Friday midnight = Thursday evening US/UK. DistroKid releases at midnight in each timezone — plan content around NZT first.
