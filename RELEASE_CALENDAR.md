## NZST Release Time

> For NZ-based releases, Friday at midnight NZST is the global release standard.
> DistroKid releases globally at midnight in each timezone (rolling release).
> To capture NZ listeners first, be aware your track may go live Thursday night in some markets.

| Time Zone | Release Moment |
|-----------|---------------|
| NZST (UTC+12/13) | Friday 12:00 AM |
| AEST (UTC+10/11) | Thursday 10:00–11:00 PM |
| UTC | Thursday 11:00 AM |
| PST (UTC-8) | Thursday 3:00 AM |
| EST (UTC-5) | Thursday 6:00 AM |
