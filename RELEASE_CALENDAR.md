## Release Strategy Notes

- Singles: every 4–8 weeks for consistent algorithm presence
- EP: 4–6 tracks; release after 2–3 singles establish the sound
- Album: 8–12 tracks; minimum 6 months after EP
- Always pitch Spotify editorial ≥ 21 days before release date (21 days gives best shot at Friday New Music)
- Target release day: **Friday** (global New Music Friday)
- Never release on major holiday weekends (Christmas, New Year) unless intentional
