## 1. Playlist Submission Strategy

### Spotify Editorial Playlists
- **Submit via:** Spotify for Artists (artists.spotify.com)
- **Submit when:** 7–21 days before release date
- **How to pitch:**
  - Claim your Spotify Artist profile first
  - In the pitch, describe: mood, genre, instrumentation, key instruments, BPM, story
  - Be specific — "dark electronic with distorted 808s and cinematic pads at 140 BPM" beats "electronic music"
- **Target playlists:** Start with algorithmic (Release Radar, Discover Weekly) — these are automatic. Pitch to editorial for larger reach.

### Independent Playlist Curators
Submit to independent curators via these platforms (all free tiers available):

| Platform | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| SubmitHub | Paid credits for guaranteed listens; free submissions available |
| Groover | Paid; curators commit to listen and respond |
| Playlist Push | Paid campaign-based |
| Musosoup | Paid; good for blog/playlist combo |
| Daily Playlists | Free submission form |

**Curator Research:** Search Spotify for playlists in your genre with 1,000–50,000 followers. Find curator contact via playlist description or linked social profiles.

### SoundCloud / Mixcloud DJ Pools
- Submit mixes and tracks to SoundCloud Groups (genre-specific)
- Submit to Mixcloud curated channels for DJ sets

### Beatport / Traxsource (if applicable)
- Requires separate distribution (not via DistroKid)
- Apply for label account on Beatport or use a specialist distributor

## 3. Platform-Specific Optimisation

### Spotify
**Artist Profile:**
- [ ] Artist photo updated (high-res, on-brand)
- [ ] Artist bio written (short, punchy, third-person)
- [ ] Header image updated per release
- [ ] Pinned track set to newest release
- [ ] Artist's Pick playlist created and updated

**Algorithmic Optimisation:**
- Save rate matters — ask fans to "heart" the track, not just stream
- Playlist adds matter — ask fans to add to their own playlists
- Complete listening matters — structure tracks so listeners don't skip

**Spotify for Artists Tasks:**
- [ ] Track pitched to editorial 7+ days before release
- [ ] Canvas video uploaded (3–8s loop, vertical)
- [ ] Lyrics uploaded (via DistroKid or Musixmatch)

### Apple Music
**Artist Profile:**
- [ ] Apple Music for Artists account claimed
- [ ] Artist photo and bio updated
- [ ] MusicKit integration if embedding on SongPal website

**Discoverability:**
- Apple Music favours complete metadata — fill in every field
- Submit to Apple Music's editorial team via your distributor (DistroKid has a "Stores" option)

### YouTube Music
**Channel Setup:**
- [ ] YouTube channel name matches artist name: DJ Zynrose
- [ ] Channel art and banner on-brand
- [ ] Auto-delivery from DistroKid active

**Content Strategy:**
- Upload official audio visualiser or lyric video for each track
- Create a playlist per release (single, EP, album)
- Use YouTube Shorts for 60s clips of tracks

### TikTok
**Profile Setup:**
- [ ] TikTok for Artists account claimed (links streaming platforms)
- [ ] Artist profile picture consistent with other platforms

**Content Strategy:**
- Hook-first: first 3 seconds must grab attention
- Show the production process — knob-turning, screen recordings of DAW, etc.
- Trend participation: map tracks to current sounds or dances
- Target 3–5 posts per week during release campaign

**Sound Strategy:**
- When DistroKid delivers to TikTok, claim your sound
- Encourage creators to use your sound (reach out to micro-influencers)

### SoundCloud
- Upload stems or DJ-friendly versions for community engagement
- SoundCloud Go+ subscribers get full plays — ensure DistroKid delivers here
- Engage with reposts and comments actively in first 48 hours

### Instagram / Threads
- Reels get the most algorithmic push — prioritise short video clips
- Stories for daily/casual content during campaign window
- Threads for text-based behind-the-scenes and music conversation

## 5. DJ Pool & Sync Licensing

### DJ Pool Distribution
- Beatport, Traxsource, Juno Download — require separate distribution
- DJcity — good for promotional DJ copies
- BPMSupreme — US-focused DJ pool

### Sync Licensing (TV, Film, Ads)
- Register tracks with APRA AMCOS (NZ/AU collecting society)
- Consider Musicbed, Artlist, Epidemic Sound for non-exclusive licensing income
- For exclusive sync, approach music supervisors directly with instrumental versions ready
