## 3. PLAYLIST SUBMISSION STRATEGY

### Priority 1 — Spotify Editorial Pitching
- Submit via **Spotify for Artists** ≥ 7 days before release
- Fill in: mood, genre, instrumentation, city, story
- Recommend pitching to: Electronic, New Music Friday, Chill Vibes

### Priority 2 — Independent Playlist Curators

Use **Submithub** (paid) and **PlaylistPush** (paid) for reach.  
Use **SubmitHub Free Credits** for budget-conscious submissions.

Target playlist categories by genre:
- Electronic / House → "Deep House Relax", "Electronic Essentials"
- Lo-fi / Chill → "Lo-fi Beats", "Chill Electronic"
- Workout / Energy → "Workout Electronic", "Gym Beats"
- Study → "Study With Me", "Focus Flow"

### Priority 3 — User-Generated / Blog Playlists
- Find active curators on **Soundplate** and **Indiemono**
- Research Spotify playlists via **Chartmetric** or **SpotOnTrack**
- DM curators directly with personalised pitch (not mass DM)

### Curator Pitch Email Template

```
Subject: New Track Submission — DJ Zynrose "[Track Title]" ([Genre])

Hi [Curator Name],

I'm DJ Zynrose, a NZ-based electronic music producer. My new track 
"[Track Title]" is releasing [DATE] and I think it could fit your

Here's the pre-release / streaming link: [link]
```
